I feel the need to justify myself sometimes. This may help you recognize the meme songs for what they are.

==
**Dave Rodgers, Initial D** - songs from an anime about car racing that became the poster children for 'really high-tempo eurobeat' and the Eurobeat genre in general.
==
**FinFin Come and See Him** - Finfin on Teo the Magic Planet is an obscure computer game that the streamer collective Radio TV Solutions discovered and thus highlighted to the world. I don't know if this song was made for the game, or if it's a fansong.
